Welcome to HHCL

Hughes & Hughes Chem Ltd. (HHCL) is a leading Indian company specializing in industrial and institutional cleaning solutions. Founded with a mission to revolutionize hygiene practices, we provide eco-friendly and effective chemical products for various sectors including aviation, defence, healthcare, railways, and urban sanitation.

We are known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. Our products meet the highest standards and are used by top organizations across the country. Whether it's disinfectants, degreasers, or specialty chemicals, HHCL is your trusted partner in cleanliness and compliance.

We believe in creating long-term value for our clients, employees, and society by maintaining transparency, delivering consistent performance, and following ethical business practices.
